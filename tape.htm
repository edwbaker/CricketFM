Tape collection digitisation

Preserving the NHM sound archive

The Natural History Museum collection of more than 900 reel-to-reel tapes has been digitised and made available on bio.acousti.ca. In addition, we have digitised collections from vinyl records, Compact Discs, cassette tapes, Digital Audio Tapes, and MiniDiscs - preserving digital audio for future generations.
